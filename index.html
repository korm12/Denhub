SERVICES

Braces are devices used in orthodontic that align and straighten teeth and help position them with regard to a person's bite while improving dental health
Dental Check-up allows your dentist to see if you have any dental problems and helps you keep your mouth healthy that will help to prevent posible problems.
Dental Cleaning is part of oral hygine and involves the removal of dental plaque from the teeth with the intention of preventing caveties and diseases.
Dental Extraction is the removal of the teeth from the dental alveolus in the alveolar bone. Teeth are often removed when they are damaged or impacted.
Dental Filling is a treatment to restore the function, integrity, and morphology as well as to the replacement of such structure supported by dental implants
Dentures are prostethic devices constructed to replace the missing teeth, and are supported by the sorrounding soft tissue and hard tissue of the cavity.
